Document to Markdown Converter - Beta


Local on-device converting only!
Supports:
  • PDF (.pdf)
  • PowerPoint (.pptx)
  • Word (.docx)
  • Excel (.xlsx)
  • WIP: Images (EXIF metadata, and OCR)
  • WIP: Audio (EXIF metadata, and speech transcription)
  • HTML (special handling of Wikipedia, etc.)
  • Various other text-based formats (csv, json, xml, etc.)
Based on MarkItDown, executed as on-device WASM via Pyodide.
